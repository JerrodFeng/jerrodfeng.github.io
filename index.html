Biography

I am currently an associate professor in the Software College of Northeastern University (NEU). I received my Ph.D degree from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) under supervison of Prof. Huamin Qu and Prof. Shuang-hua Yang. I received my B.Eng. degree from Northeastern University (China) and MSc. degree from Loughborough University (UK) in 2017 and 2018, respectively.
My research interests focus on big data analytics, data visualization, explainable machine learning and urban computing.
